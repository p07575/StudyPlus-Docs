## Quick Start

### 1. Create an Account

Your school administrator will provide you with an **invite code** to register. Visit [studypl.us](https://studypl.us) and click **Sign Up** to get started.

### 2. Connect Your eClass

Once logged in, navigate to **eClass** in the sidebar and enter your school credentials. Choose how you want to save your login (encrypted recommended). We only read your data - we never modify anything on eClass.

### 3. Start Studying Smarter

Your assignments and calendar will sync from eClass. Use the platform to:
- Track homework completion status
- View your school calendar
- Break down assignments into manageable tasks with AI
- Generate study materials with SAILS
