# Getting Started

Welcome to **Studyember** - your all-in-one study companion that helps you stay organized and on top of your assignments.

## What is Studyember?

Studyember is a student study management platform that integrates with your school's eClass system to automatically sync your:

- 📝 **Homework assignments** - Never miss a deadline
- 📅 **Calendar events** - Stay organized with synced schedules  
- ✅ **To-do lists** - Manage tasks with AI-powered breakdown
- 🤖 **SAILS** - AI study tools for material generation

## Quick Start

### 1. Create an Account

Your school administrator will provide you with an **invite code** to register. Visit [studypl.us](https://studypl.us) and click **Sign Up** to get started.

### 2. Connect Your eClass

Once logged in, navigate to **eClass** in the sidebar and enter your school credentials. Choose how you want to save your login (encrypted recommended). We only read your data - we never modify anything on eClass.

### 3. Start Studying Smarter

Your assignments and calendar will sync from eClass. Use the platform to:
- Track homework completion status
- View your school calendar
- Break down assignments into manageable tasks with AI
- Generate study materials with SAILS

## Features Overview

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **eClass Sync** | Homework, calendar, and records sync |
| **Homework Tracking** | Mark assignments complete and track progress |
| **SAILS** | AI-powered study material and task generation |
| **Dashboard** | Weather widget and quick stats |
| **To-Do List** | Task management with reordering |
