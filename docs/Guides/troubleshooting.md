# Troubleshooting

Common issues and how to fix them.

## Login Issues

### Can't Log In

**Problem**: Unable to log into StudyPlus

**Solutions**:
1. Check your email and password are correct
2. Clear browser cache and cookies
3. Try a different browser
4. Check if your account is verified (check email)

### Session Expired

**Problem**: Getting logged out frequently

**Solutions**:
1. Ensure cookies are enabled in your browser
2. Don't use incognito/private mode
3. Check if your browser blocks third-party cookies

## eClass Connection

### Connection Failed

**Problem**: Can't connect to eClass

**Solutions**:
1. Verify your eClass credentials are correct
2. Check if eClass is accessible (try logging in directly)
3. Your school's eClass might be under maintenance
4. Try disconnecting and reconnecting

### Data Not Syncing

**Problem**: Assignments or calendar not updating

**Solutions**:
1. Click the manual sync/refresh button
2. Wait 15 minutes for automatic sync
3. Check if the items exist on eClass
4. Disconnect and reconnect your eClass account

## Performance

### Slow Loading

**Problem**: Pages loading slowly

**Solutions**:

2. Clear browser cache
3. Disable browser extensions
4. Try a different browser
