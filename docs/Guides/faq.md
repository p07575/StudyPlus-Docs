# FAQ

Frequently asked questions about StudyPlus.

## General

### What is StudyPlus?

StudyPlus is a student study management platform that integrates with your school's eClass system to help you stay organized with assignments, deadlines, and study materials.

### Is StudyPlus free?

StudyPlus is available to students at registered schools. Contact your school administrator or reach out to [sales@studypl.us](mailto:sales@studypl.us) to get access.

### Which schools are supported?

StudyPlus currently supports schools using eClass systems. Contact us to check if your school is compatible.

## Account

### How do I create an account?

Accounts are created through your school. Contact your school administrator or our sales team for access.

### Can I change my email address?

Yes, go to **Settings** → **Account** → **Change Email**. You'll need to verify the new email address.

### How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the email instructions.

## eClass

### Is my eClass password safe?

Yes! Your credentials are encrypted with AES-256 encryption and stored securely. Our team cannot view your passwords.

### Does StudyPlus modify my eClass data?

No. StudyPlus only **reads** data from eClass. We cannot submit assignments, change grades, or modify any content.

### Why are some assignments missing?

- The assignment may not be published yet on eClass
- Try a manual sync
- The course may not be enrolled properly

## Features

### How does the AI task generator work?

Paste your assignment description, and our AI breaks it down into smaller, actionable tasks with suggested time estimates.

### Can I export my data?

Yes, go to **Settings** → **Export Data** to download your tasks, calendar, and study materials.

### Does StudyPlus work offline?

Currently, StudyPlus requires an internet connection. Offline mode is planned for a future update.

## Privacy

### What data does StudyPlus collect?

We collect account information (email, name), synced eClass data, and anonymous usage analytics. See our [Privacy & Security](/docs/guides/privacy) page for details.

### How do I delete my account?

Go to **Settings** → **Delete Account**. All your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

## Technical

### Which browsers are supported?

- Chrome (recommended)
- Firefox
- Safari
- Edge

### Is there a mobile app?

StudyPlus is a web app that works on mobile browsers. A native app is planned for the future.

### Why do I keep getting logged out?

Ensure cookies are enabled and you're not using incognito mode. Sessions last 90 days under normal conditions.
