# Privacy & Security

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Account info** | Email, name for authentication |
| **eClass data** | Homework, calendar (read-only sync) |
| **Usage data** | App analytics to improve service |

## eClass Security

:::info Read-Only Access
StudyPlus only **reads** data from eClass. We cannot and will never:
- Submit assignments
- Change grades
- Modify any eClass content
- Post messages
:::

### Credential Storage

Your eClass credentials are:
- Encrypted with AES-256 encryption
- Stored securely on our servers
- Never accessible to our team
- Deletable at any time from Settings

## Data Storage

- All data stored in secure, encrypted databases
- Servers located in secure data centers
- Regular security audits and updates
- HTTPS encryption for all connections

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Access** - View all your stored data
2. **Export** - Download your data anytime
3. **Delete** - Remove your account and all data
4. **Correct** - Update inaccurate information

## Account Deletion

To delete your account and all data:

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Click **Delete Account**
3. Confirm deletion
4. All data is permanently removed within 30 days
